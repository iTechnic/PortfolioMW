This site collects almost nothing. Here is exactly what happens to any information you send, in plain English.

What gets collected

If you use the contact form or email me, I receive what you send: your name, email address, business name, and your message. That is the only personal information this site collects.

  • No analytics or tracking scripts run on this site.
  • No cookies are set.
  • No advertising or social media pixels are present.

How it gets used

Your details are used to reply to your enquiry and to work on your project if we go ahead. They are never sold, shared for marketing, or added to a mailing list.

Who processes it

Form submissions are delivered to my inbox by FormSubmit, a form-to-email service, and the site is hosted on Vercel. Both process the data only to provide those services.

How long it is kept

Enquiry emails stay in my inbox so I can pick up a conversation where it left off. If you want your details deleted, email me and I will remove them.

Your rights

You can ask what information I hold about you, ask for a copy, or ask for it to be deleted at any time: makswittenbecksites@gmail.com

Last updated July 2026. If how this site handles data changes, this page changes with it.
