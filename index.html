How it works

See it before you spend anything.

You do not need a brief or a finished plan. The first thing you judge is a real website, built around your business.

1

Send me your idea

Tell me what your business does and what the site should achieve. A few sentences is plenty to start.

2

I build a demo concept

Within days you get a link to a short working version of your site, designed around your business. No cost, no obligation.

3

We refine it together

You tell me what feels right and what does not. Design, words, photos, and structure get adjusted until it feels like yours.

4

You approve and pay

Happy with the direction? You get a fixed price, you pay, and the full build begins. You never pay for something you have not seen.

5

I build and launch it

Every page finished, polished for phones and desktops, the technical setup handled, and the site goes live.
