Questions people ask first.

How does the demo-first process work?

You describe your business, I build a short working version of your website, and you review it on a real link. If you like the direction, we refine it together, you approve it, pay, and I build the full site.

Do I have to pay before seeing anything?

No. The demo comes first, at no cost and with no obligation. Payment happens once, after you approve the direction, and always against a fixed written quote.

What kind of websites can you build?

Business websites, landing pages, booking and enquiry pages, and redesigns. The live work covers a web app, a plumber, a barber, a cleaner, and a pizzeria, and the same range applies to your trade.

Can you build more than a basic website?

Yes. When you need it, I can add online payments and Stripe checkout, customer logins and account areas, sign-up and booking systems, newsletters and email capture, and a simple dashboard so you can manage content and prices yourself. A site can start simple and grow as the business does.

Can I request changes?

Yes, that is the point of the demo stage. Layout, words, colours, photos, structure: you say what feels wrong and it gets adjusted before anything is agreed.

How long does a website take?

The first demo lands within days. The full build usually takes one to two weeks after approval, depending on size and how quickly content comes together.

Will the website work on phones?

Every site here is designed at phone width first, then expanded to desktop. Open any of the live projects on your phone and check the claim yourself.

Can you improve my current website?

Yes. I build a sharper version alongside your existing site so you can compare the two side by side before deciding anything.

What happens after I approve the demo?

You get a fixed quote, you pay, and the demo grows into the complete site: every page finished, your real content in place, tested on real devices, and launched.

Can you help with domain, hosting, and launch?

Yes, the technical setup is part of the job. I can register the domain with you, set up fast hosting, connect your email, and get the site live. You keep ownership of all of it.

Do I need my content ready?

No. A short description of the business is enough to start, and the demo uses placeholder material where needed. Photos, wording, and details get filled in as we refine.
