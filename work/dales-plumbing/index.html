Dales Plumbing & Heating

A demo built for a fictional Harrogate plumbing and heating firm. The brief I set myself: someone has water coming through the ceiling, they are on their phone, and they need to decide who to call in under a minute.

The brief

Trade websites fail in a predictable way: a wall of text, a buried phone number, and stock photos that could belong to anyone. The customer usually arrives stressed and in a hurry.

The site had to surface proof fast, rating, reviews, years trading, Gas Safe registration, and make calling effortless at every scroll position.

What I built

  • Photo hero with the rating badge and phone number above the fold
  • Sticky mobile call bar so the number is always one thumb away
  • Service sections for boilers, bathrooms, repairs, and emergencies
  • Trust stats, a featured review, and an FAQ with structured data
  • An areas-covered section for local search intent

Design decisions

Every trust point on the page maps to something a real firm could prove: review counts, certifications, years trading. Nothing is invented decoration, because customers check.

The palette is steady blue with one warm accent for the call action. On a trade site the call button is the entire business, so nothing else competes with it.
